Idea Day 2020 Canceled

On Thursday, Gov. DeWine issued a mandate to close all K-12 schools starting at the end of the day Monday, March 16 through Friday, April 3. Because Idea Day would fall within this time frame, we regret to inform you that Idea Day 2020 has been cancelled.

SIGNUPS:


  • This year we will be piloting a new platform for signups called Sched.
  • You will get an email from Sched inviting you to set up your account sign in information
  • You need to set up your account before Friday if you want to sign up early.
  • Additionally, we’ll push out a UA Idea Day app to make sign ups easier.
  • The icon will look like the image on the right:
  • Once you sign up, you can access your schedule anytime, with all session details included.
  • Session types will be color-coded:
    • 2.5 hour workshops are colored purple
    • 1 hour sprints are colored yellow
    • ½ day field trips are light blue
    • Full day workshops are pink
    • Full day field trips are dark blue
  • Once you click on a session you are in it. There is NO SUBMIT BUTTON. If you try to choose a session that does not fit into your schedule, you will be prompted to choose something else.
  • Make sure you fill your schedule! Otherwise we will auto-assign you to a session.
  • Last year, some of the students who chose two field trips were late to the afternoon experience. For that reason, we ask you to choose only one field trip.
For more information, go to the help page!
