Countdown to Idea Day 2020 (April 3)

Welcome

UA Idea Day is an event created by students, teachers, and our partners at the Upper Arlington Education Foundation in order to inspire us all to dream about how we can use our skills to forge change, innovate, and —in some way—make the world a better place.

This year, your schedule is entirely customizable with over 80 different session options centered on the theme of Kaleidoscope: A New Perspective.
